Description:

A juicy and scrumptious recipie for baked chicken breast! Enjoy the spicy flavor of the seasonings from this simple recipie.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium size chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp of the following: cayenne pepper, paprika, and cajun powder
  • 1 tsp of the following: salt, pepper, and sugar
  • cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 390 degrees Farenheit
  2. Mix the seasonings together into a small bowl.
  3. Score the chicken breasts and drizzle 2 tbsp of oil onto them. Coat all sides of the meat evenly.
  4. Using a spoon, sprinkle the seasonings on top of each side and rub them over. Make sure all surface is covered.
  5. Lay the meat side by side on a sheet pan and place it in the oven for 15 minutes.
  6. After 15 minutes, flip the chicken breasts on the other side to cook for an additional 10 minutes.
  7. After 25 minutes, take the chicken out and let them cool before serving.
