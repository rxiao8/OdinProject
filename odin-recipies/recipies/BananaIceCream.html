Description:

Delicious and healthy dessert that is super simple to make. Enjoy a smooth and creamy banana ice cream on a hot summer day!

Ingredients:

  • 2 bananas
  • 1/2 milk (dairy or vegan)
  • 1/4 nuts (of your choice)
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Slice the bananas and throw in the freezer for at least four hours
  2. Take out the bananas and throw them in a blender with milk and vanilla extract.
  3. Blend until smooth texture
  4. Scope onto bowls and top off with your choice of nuts
